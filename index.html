About Us

Our Story, Our Purpose

8+
Years of Service

Building a Sustainable Future from the Ground Up

Bhavprita Seva Samiti was established in 2015 with a simple yet powerful vision — to create a world where every community has access to education, every woman has the power to shape her destiny, and every child grows up surrounded by nature's bounty.

What started as a small group of passionate individuals in a single village has now grown into a movement touching over 50 villages across tribal and rural regions. Our holistic approach addresses the interconnected challenges of environmental degradation, illiteracy, and gender inequality.

Our Mission

To empower rural communities through education, environmental stewardship, and inclusive development programs.

Our Vision

A self-reliant, educated, and environmentally conscious society where every individual thrives with dignity.

Founder's Message
"Every tree we plant, every student we encourage, every woman we empower — these aren't just programs. They are seeds of a revolution that will bloom for generations to come."

Our Programs

What We Do

Five focused programs creating multi-dimensional impact across rural and tribal communities.

Environmental Protection & Awareness

We conduct tree plantation drives, waste management workshops, and environmental awareness campaigns in schools and communities. Our programs have led to the planting of over 15,000 trees and the creation of 5 community gardens.

Tree Plantation Waste Management Awareness Camps

Adult Education
प्रौढ़ शिक्षा

Literacy classes for adults in rural communities, covering reading, writing, basic numeracy, and digital literacy. Over 800 adults have become literate through our centers.

Student Motivation Program

Regular visits to government schools with career counseling sessions, mentorship programs, and inspirational talks to encourage students to pursue higher education.

Matric Topper Awards

We honor and reward top-performing students of government school matric examinations with cash prizes, medals, and certificates to motivate academic excellence.

Tribal Women Empowerment

Skill development, self-help group formation, vocational training, and leadership programs designed specifically for tribal women to achieve financial independence and social dignity.
